Winter School 2021

Manufacturing in Mars

The 2021 winter school implemented a challenge-based approach; Participants were asked to collaboratively address a societal challenge and creatively use their scientific/technology knowledge to propose a solution, whilst developing their leadership, innovation and entrepreneurship skills.

  • The first element was an online onboarding starting between mid-October to mid-November. The onboarding was a fully digital set of SPOCs and online workshops to bring all participants to a level playing field in the innovation, entrepreneurship and scientific/technological aspects required for the winter school challenge..
  • The second element was based on on-line collaboration and mentoring.

Between the onboarding and the on-site activities, the teams worked on their challenge remotely in “virtual Hackathons” - This was done collaboratively with the support of designated mentors and provided material for the on-site part of the winter school.

  • The third and final element was the on-site event with a duration of one week that took place in Porto.

The winter school culminated in a networking afternoon where the participants present their results and get exposure to investors and potential clients.
